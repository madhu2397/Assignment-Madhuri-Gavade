Time line of Abdul Kalam's Life

  • Birth - born in Rameswaram, Madras Presidency, British India (present-day Tamil Nadu, India).
  • Family - Their father Jainulabdeen Marakayar was a boat owner and imam of a local mosque; their mother Ashiamma was a housewife.
  • Schooling - He started his schooling from Schwartz Higher School in Ramanathpuram and graduation in Physics from University of Madras and then study Aerospace technology from Madras institute of technology
  • Career - Dr.kalam started his career as a scientist from thr Aeronautical Development Estlablishment of DRDO and worked under eminent scientist Vikram Sarabha.
  • Career Life - He started his contribution as a scientist in 1959 when he was the project director of India's first satellite Launch vehicle(SLV-III) was used to launch the satellite ROhini in 1980 and is considered the developer of the Polar satellite Launch vehicle.
  • Missile Man- He also played a crucial role in the development of Agni and Prithvi missiles.
  • Chief Scientific Adviser - Dr. Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister in 1992 when the Pokhran-II nuclear tests were conducted.
  • President of India - In 2002, Dr. Kalam was elected as the 11th President of India.
  • People's President - Dr.Kalam had received the title of "People's President" During his lenure as president,he had rejected the mercy plea of the Dhananjoy Chatterjee, convicted of brutal rape and murder.
  • Bharat Ratna -He was only the 3rd president to be awarded the Bharat Ratna.
  • Academics - However he do not file for Presidency for a second term instead returned to the field of academics and continued to do so till his death in 2015.
  • Books and Publications - "Wings of fire", "Ignited minds", "Target 3 billion","Transcendence", "Indomitable Spirit".
